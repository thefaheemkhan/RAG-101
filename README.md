# AI Workflow Automation Roadmap

A complete roadmap and learning repository for mastering **AI Workflow Automation** — from beginner concepts to advanced AI agent systems.

This repository is designed for:
- Beginners entering AI automation
- Developers building AI workflows
- Freelancers & agencies
- AI engineers
- Startup founders
- Anyone interested in automating real-world work using AI

---

# What is AI Workflow Automation?

AI Workflow Automation combines:
- Artificial Intelligence
- APIs
- Automation tools
- Logic systems
- AI agents
- Business workflows

to automate repetitive, intelligent, and multi-step tasks.

Examples:
- AI customer support systems
- Lead qualification automation
- AI email assistants
- AI content pipelines
- AI research workflows
- AI operations systems
- Multi-agent automations
